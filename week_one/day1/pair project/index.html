What?!

Seriously. Without coffee, there would be:

Everyone would be sad. And tired. Some people don't like coffee. Our mission is to change that. How? The answer is simple: We make people drink coffee. Our belief is that if people drink enough coffee, then eventually they will enjoy it. Here are the methods we will use: Introducing coffee to drinking water sources such as reservoirs, aquifers, etc. Offering tax incentives to coffee drinkers. Intravenous coffee supplied to those who refuse to drink it.
