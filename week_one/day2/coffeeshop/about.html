About Us

We are dedicated to providing discount coffee. Our primary mission is to ensure that everyone in America consumes an adequate amount of coffee every day. Forever.

Why?

Coffee is the single greatest invention mankind has ever created.

What?!

Without coffee, there would be:

  • No great works of literature.
  • No computers. Therefore, no Internet.
  • No coffee tables.
  • No coffee cake (probably no biscotti, either).
  • No art.
  • No happiness.

Everyone would be sad. And tired. Some people don't like coffee. Our mission is to change that.

How?

The answer is simple: We make people drink coffee. Our belief is that if people drink enough coffee, then eventually they will enjoy it. Here are the methods we will use:

  1. Introducing coffee to drinking water sources such as reservoirs, aquifers, etc.
  2. Offering tax incentives to coffee drinkers.
  3. Intravenous coffee supplied to those who refuse to drink it.
